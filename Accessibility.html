Accessibility

Alt tags - shows text when mouseover on a picture/video, mostly used for people that use a screen reader due to visual impairments
Include subtitles in videos for people with visual impairments.
Using abbreviations for phrases such as the Central Intelligence Agency. (C.I.A) as a screen reader will spell it out phonetically.
Colour control. Make sure to choose colour scheme well. Too many colours slightly the same can be confusing for colour blind people
Clickable. Try to make any thing that can be clicked on relatively big as it can cause issues with someone that has a mobility issue.
Keep your copy simple. If there is a lot of text try and break it into bulletpoints. This will help if any of your audience is elderly or have learning difficulties.
